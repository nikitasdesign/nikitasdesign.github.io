Intro

Flowers not only beautify everyday life, they also make wonderful gifts. But flowers are not only beautiful, behind most of them there is also a message that you can use specifically for the occasion. We wanted to design a sustainable flower shop that sells unique bouquets with self-specified messages and delivers them environmentally friendly by bike.

Empathize & Define

Pain points

  • shipping is bad for the environment and flowers
  • same day delivery goes wrong/very expensive
  • gifting yourself flowers takes out the fun, no surprise like gift from others
  • meaningless flowers as gifts are not personal

Minimum viable product

  • Simple and fast way to order beautiful flowers
  • support the local flowershops by picking up the flowers there or getting them delivered by bike

Unique selling point

  • ordering surprise flower bouquets by only choosing colors or according to a theme (love, good wishes, hope, gratefulness, support) or just totally random
  • choosing flowers with meaning - not just pretty to look at

As part of the unique selling point is to sell bouquets by their meaning we researched for different flowers and their meanings. In the end we had a big database with over 70 flowers and their meanings.
